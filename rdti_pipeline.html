Why documentation breaks firms

R&D documentation eats
your margin.

The technical write-up is the most time-consuming, least scalable part of an R&D claim. It is also where claims are won or lost.

01

Hours that don't scale

Senior consultants spend days drafting and re-checking activity descriptions, hypotheses and experiment write-ups — work that grows linearly with every new client.

02

Compliance drift

Character limits, prohibited language, structural rules and en-GB conventions are easy to breach under deadline pressure. One inconsistency with source data can undermine a claim.

03

Inconsistent quality

Output quality varies with whoever drafted it and how much time they had. Your firm's standard should not depend on the day of the week.

How it works
The pipeline

A pipeline, not a chatbot.

RDTI Pipeline runs a disciplined two-stage process before assembling the finished document. Each stage is built around how a compliant R&D application actually has to read.

i
Step 01 · Draft

Compliant draft

The agent drafts every required section — objectives, core activity, hypothesis, experiment, results, conclusions and new knowledge — compliant with language rules, character limits and bullet discipline from the first word. No clean-up pass required.

Structured technical content
ii
Step 02 · Audit

Factual audit

Every claim and figure is cross-referenced against your source materials. A visible audit list surfaces every discrepancy, and surgical corrections are applied — so nothing in the document outruns the evidence.

Verified against source
iii
Step 03 · Assemble

Assemble & deliver

The audited content is written straight into the formal R&D Tax Incentive application template, with every fillable section populated and formatting preserved. Delivered as a finished Word document, ready for your review and sign-off.

03·04
Capabilities
Engineered for compliance

Built for the way
R&D claims are judged.

01 · Compliance

Compliance built in

British English, first-person plural voice, prohibited-word filtering, punctuation rules and per-section character limits are enforced as the draft is written — not patched afterwards.

02 · Audit

Transparent factual audit

Every claim is checked against your source documents and presented as an inspectable audit list before the final document is produced. The evidence trail is visible, not hidden.

03 · Template

Straight into the template

Output lands in the official R&D Tax Incentive application format, every fillable section populated, formatting preserved — paragraphs, bullets and bold labels intact.

04 · Integrity

No invented data

Where source material is missing, the agent inserts a clearly marked placeholder instead of fabricating content — gaps are surfaced for your team, never papered over.

05 · Coverage

Supporting activities handled

Core activities and every linked supporting activity are drafted and fact-checked as separate blocks — none silently dropped, each held to the same standard.

06 · Consistency

Your standard, every time

The same disciplined output regardless of who runs it or how busy the week is. Quality stops being a function of available capacity.
